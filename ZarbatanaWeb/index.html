What is TimeAura?

TimeAura is a community-first platform designed to restore the lost art of neighborhood mutual aid—bringing back the human connection that existed before the internet. A professional cook and a talented hairdresser might live on the same floor, unaware that one wants to learn how to bake a perfect pizza, while the other needs a stylish haircut. TimeAura bridges that gap. We turn time into a currency of kindness, allowing neighbors within the same district or city to exchange services, help each other, and build real physical communities.

IMAGINE THIS...

Maria, 34, Kyiv. She's lived on the 5th floor for 3 years. She's a hairdresser. She cuts hair beautifully, but dreams of learning to bake Italian pizza.

On the 6th floor lives Andriy. A chef. He makes the best carbonara in town. But he needs a haircut — and he's too shy to go to a salon.

They live 3 meters apart. But they've never spoken.

TimeAura connected them in 4 seconds. The Oracle saw: 'Hairdresser nearby wants pizza. Chef nearby wants a haircut.'

Today, Maria is teaching Andriy how to make dough. Andriy is cutting Maria's hair on Saturday morning. They drink coffee on the balcony. They are friends.

It's not magic. It's the neighborhood we forgot.

HOW IT WORKS?

TimeAura combines a social mission with a sustainable business model.

🔹 THREE-LAYER ECONOMY

Fiat (USD/EUR)

For professional services and B2B contracts. Stripe Connect handles payments from $1 to $13,000+.

Horas (Time)

For barter and neighborhood mutual aid. 1 Hora = 60 minutes of work. Zero fees.
